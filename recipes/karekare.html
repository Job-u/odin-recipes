Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 lb oxtail, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup annatto oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours or until the oxtail is tender.
  3. Serve hot with steamed rice.
