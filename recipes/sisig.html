Sisig

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly, diced
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, soy sauce, oil, garlic, and black pepper in a bowl.
  2. Marinate the pork in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat and cook the pork until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice.
